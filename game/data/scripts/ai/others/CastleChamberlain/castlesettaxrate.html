[Adjust Tax Rate]
Today's tax rate is %tax_rate%% and tomorrow's tax rate will be %next_tax_rate%%.
The tax rate may be set in the range of 0% ~ %tax_limit%%. The tax rate entered will be applied from midnight of the same day.
Please note that the tax rate will be set to 0% if the "OK" button is pressed without any value being entered.
